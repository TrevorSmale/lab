Individual Components

The stator is the heart of the motor, it concentrates the electromagnetic energy into rotational energy.

The transfer case transmitts the rotational energy of the stator into other dynamic elements.

The shaft transmitts the directed rotational energy and torque from the motor to the transmission

The Pulley is the transmission interface within the transfer case.

Maintanence Schedule

Rebuilds must be done after 100,000 Miles.

The transfer case needs lubrication on bearings every 20,000 Miles.

Pulleys stretch and need replacement every 80,000 Miles.
